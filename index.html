5 of the most common dog breeds with information.

Not everyone can own a dog, but some people like to know about them. I personally like to know about all kinds of dog breeds, so here is 5 of the more common dog breeds in America, and some information about them!

Information

Breed: Labrador Retriever
Lifespan: 10-12 years
Height: 54-57 cm
Weight: 65-80lbs
Additional Information: This breed of dog is liked amongst many people in America. The breed originated from Newfoundland in Canada and is known for being super swimmers, handling the coldest of water for extensive periods of time. Labrador Retrievers are typically one of the original breeds that have made many of the retriever breeds we know today.

Information

Breed:French Bulldog
Lifespan: 10-12 years
Height: 28-33 cm
Weight: 25-35lbs
Additional Information: This breed of dog originates from a breed everyone knows, the bulldog. Even though the breed's name is "French Bulldog," the breed originated from England and not France. They are typically low energy dogs and have ears that people resemble as bat ears.

Information

Breed:German Shepherd
Lifespan: 9-13 years
Height: 55-65 cm
Weight: 65-85lbs
Additional Information:German Shepherds indeed originated from Germany, following the name unlike the past two breeds. The breed is known for being able to help with many things, being therapy or even service help. It is also essential to teach this breed to not bite on things, as they will naturally bite on things due to heritage.

Picture

Information

Breed:Golden Retriever
Lifespan: 10-12 years
Height: 51-61 cm
Weight: 60-70lbs
Additional Information:The Golden Retriever was a breed from water spaniel and original retriever breeds, typically being Labrador Retrievers. Golden Retrievers are known to be guide and hearing dogs for the blind and deaf. The furcoats on this breed have their own features. The topcoat is water-resistant, and the undercoat helps cool the body of the dog, which means that the dog is able to keep a comfortable temperature through each season.

Picture

Information

Breed:Poodle
Lifespan: 10-15 years
Height: 45-60 cm
Weight: 45-80lbs
Additional Information:Poodles are a very diverse breed of dog. There are many different types of poodles, explaining the huge range in height and weight. Poodles have a single coat of fur, which doesn't shed much compared to other dog breeds. Poodles are also able to be trained to ignore gunfire, making them eligible for battlefield tasks. They are also very big sleepers. They can sleep up to 13 hours a day to be fully energized and refreshed.
